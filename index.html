Calcoolator

By Vlardez Enterprises

You can press the buttons or use the keyboard to enter in numbers. This calcoolator will perform operations on two numbers at a time, and does not follow the order of operations. You can press Backspace to delete numbers from the display one at a time, or press Delete to fully clear/reset the display. Pressing Enter or the Equal key will evaluate the current operation. You can also press the +, -, *, / and % keys to perform operations. Have fun!
